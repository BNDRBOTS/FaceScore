Refund Policy

FaceScore Mirror is a digital product delivered through the configured storefront. Refund handling follows the rules and transaction tools of the storefront used for purchase.

Before purchasing

A timed free demo (60 seconds) is provided so you can verify browser compatibility, camera permission, interface usability, and the nature of the measurements before buying. We strongly recommend trying the demo first.

Technical problems

If the product cannot run in a supported modern browser over HTTPS or localhost, use the configured support link and include the browser, device, and error shown. Reasonable troubleshooting or replacement access will be provided where possible. The "Notify BNDR.Labs" button (visible after an unrecoverable failure) sends a sanitized diagnostic that helps us reproduce and fix the issue faster.

Non-refundable circumstances

Refunds may be declined where the product functions as described, an access code has been publicly shared or abused, or a request falls outside the applicable Stripe or Gumroad refund process.

Upgrades

If you upgrade from Mirror Pro to Mirror Studio, contact support with both purchase receipts and we will refund the Pro purchase so you only pay the difference.

Nothing here removes legal rights

This policy does not limit mandatory consumer rights that apply in your jurisdiction.

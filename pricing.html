PRICING FAQ

Common questions.

Why one-time instead of a subscription?

Because there's no server to maintain. The model runs in your browser. There's no ongoing compute cost to justify a monthly bill. You pay once, you own it.

What happens if I lose my access code?

Use the support link in the footer (when configured) and include your purchase receipt. We'll re-issue a code. Because access is browser-local, you'll need to enter the new code on each device you want to use.

Can I upgrade from Pro to Studio later?

Yes. Buy Studio at the regular price, then contact support with both receipts. We'll refund the Pro purchase so you only pay the difference.

Is the commercial-use license required to use it on my clients?

If you use FaceScore Mirror in a paid service — makeup artistry, photography consultations, coaching — yes, the Studio tier's commercial license covers that. The Pro tier is for personal use.

Do you offer refunds?

Refunds follow the rules of the storefront you purchased through (Stripe or Gumroad). If the product cannot run in a supported modern browser over HTTPS, contact support with the browser, device, and error shown. Full details are on the Refund Policy page.

Why is the demo only 60 seconds?

Long enough to verify browser compatibility, camera permission, interface usability, and the nature of the measurements. Short enough that the demo can't be used in place of purchase. After 60 seconds, you can still explore the interface without the camera.

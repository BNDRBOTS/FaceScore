FAQ

Straight answers.

Does FaceScore upload my camera image?

No. The model runs locally in the browser. The app does not include an upload endpoint.

Is this a beauty or attractiveness score?

No. The composite is a weighted geometry reference. It is not an objective judgment of appearance or worth.

Why does the score sometimes disappear?

The app withholds measurements when face angle, size, lighting, or image clarity is not sufficient.

Can I use it on a phone?

Yes. It is mobile-first and works in modern browsers over HTTPS. Camera permissions are controlled by your device.

What is saved?

Essential access status and, with permission, preferences and metric-only history. No camera frame is saved.

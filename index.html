WHAT IT MEASURES

Geometry, not judgment.

Three live measurements plus a composite reference. All from one camera view, all on your device.

⊡

Bilateral balance

Compares paired landmarks after correcting eye-line rotation and face scale. Reported as 0–100 with the residual deviation shown beside it.

φ

Proportion reference

Compares face-height and cheek-width geometry with a 1.618 reference ratio. Reported as 0–100; the measured ratio is shown for transparency.

◈

Expression dynamics

Tracks internal eye, brow, and mouth motion after reducing rigid head movement. Movement intensity only — not emotion, diagnosis, or deception.

Important: FaceScore Mirror is an educational geometry visualization. It does not measure beauty, identity, health, personality, emotion, truthfulness, or mental state.

FAQ

Straight answers.

Does FaceScore upload my camera image?

No. The model runs locally in the browser. The app does not include an upload endpoint. Your camera frames stay on your device.

Is this a beauty or attractiveness score?

No. The composite is a weighted geometry reference. It is not an objective judgment of appearance or worth. The app says so directly in its disclaimer.

Why does the score sometimes disappear?

The app withholds measurements when face angle, size, lighting, or image clarity is not sufficient. This is intentional — it refuses to show numbers it cannot stand behind.

Can I use it on a phone?

Yes. It is mobile-first and works in modern Safari on iPhone/iPad and modern Chrome, Edge, or Firefox on Android. Camera permissions are controlled by your device.

What is saved?

Essential storage keeps access status and demo usage. With your permission, optional storage remembers interface preferences and metric-only history (numbers, timestamps). No camera frame is ever saved.

What's the difference between Pro and Studio?

Pro ($29 one-time) unlocks unlimited analysis, local history, JSON/CSV export, printable reports, and priority email support. Studio ($59 one-time) adds a commercial-use license, branded reports, extended history, early access to new metrics, and a dedicated support channel. Both are lifetime — no subscription.

Can I gift FaceScore to someone?

Yes. The owner can generate SHA-256 access codes from any code string and add the hash to the app's configuration. The recipient enters the code in Settings → Access code and unlocks Pro or Studio instantly. See the README for the exact command.

What if it doesn't work in my browser?

The app gracefully reports blocked permission, unavailable WebGL or WebAssembly, missing model assets, timeout, and camera-in-use states. If a failure is genuinely unrecoverable, a one-click "Notify BNDR.Labs" button sends a sanitized diagnostic to the developer (with your permission) and shows a simple acknowledgment — never raw logs or your image.

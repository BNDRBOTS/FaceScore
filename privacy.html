Privacy Policy

FaceScore Mirror is designed to process facial geometry locally in your browser. BNDR LLC does not operate an image-upload endpoint, user account system, analytics service, or advertising tracker in this product.

Camera processing

When you grant camera permission, video frames are supplied by your browser to the bundled face-landmark model. Processing occurs on your device. Camera frames are not intentionally transmitted to BNDR LLC, stored by the app, or included in saved history.

Local browser storage

Essential local storage may retain access status, demo usage, legal acknowledgement, and storage choice. With your permission, optional storage may retain interface preferences and metric-only session history. You can delete history from the app and clear all site data through your browser.

Payments

Configured purchase buttons send you to Stripe or Gumroad. Those providers process payment information under their own privacy policies. FaceScore Mirror does not receive or store full payment-card details.

What the app does not do

The app does not identify people, compare identities, infer sensitive traits, diagnose health, detect deception, determine emotion, or objectively rate beauty or personal worth.

Children

The product is not directed to children under 13. A parent or guardian should supervise use by a minor and consider whether appearance-related measurements are appropriate.

Diagnostic reporting (optional)

If the local engine cannot recover from a failure, the app may offer a one-click "Notify BNDR.Labs" button. When you click it, a sanitized diagnostic package is sent to the configured BNDR.Labs endpoint. The package contains the failure state, sanitized environment and runtime facts, anonymized reproducible steps (action codes only — never camera data, never access codes, never user input), and a hidden AI-executable repair prompt for the repair agent. You see only a canned acknowledgment message. No raw logs, no camera frames, no access codes, and no other personally identifiable information are sent.

Contact

Use the support link displayed on the site when configured.
